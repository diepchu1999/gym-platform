## Domain Responsibilities

### Identity & RBAC
Manage login, roles, permissions, account status, and access scope by branch.

### Branch Management
Manage branches, rooms, service availability, opening rules, capacity, and branch-level configuration.

### Staff Management
Manage employees such as PT, receptionist, CSKH, cleaner, parking staff, maintenance staff, massage staff, manager, accountant, and marketing staff.

### Member Management
Manage customer profile, status, branch relation, membership history, booking history, payment history, and customer care records.

### KYC & Verification
Manage CCCD KYC, student verification, and trial eligibility.

### Membership Package
Manage trial, monthly, quarterly, yearly, VIP, student, class pass, PT session, massage add-on, and other packages.

### Contract Management
Manage contract creation, signature/confirmation, activation, expiration, termination, suspension, and attachments.

### Payment
Manage online payment, counter payment, refund, payment callback idempotency, invoices, and payment history.

### Finance Installment Integration
Manage installment application via finance provider, application status, provider approval/rejection, and disbursement status.

### QR Check-in
Manage QR token generation, validation, duplicate scan protection, access rule validation, and check-in history.

### Booking Engine
Shared booking foundation for PT, group class, private room, and massage.

### Group Class
Manage class types, sessions, instructors, rooms, capacity, class pass usage, and attendance.

### PT Booking
Manage PT profile, PT availability, 1-on-1 90-minute booking, payment, completion, and rating.

### Private Room Booking
Manage private room resources, monthly VIP quota, hourly booking, cleaning/maintenance status, and paid extra booking.

### Massage Booking
Manage VIP free quota, massage staff, massage room, booking, paid extra booking, and completion.

### Product / Inventory / POS
Manage products, partner brands, stock per branch, purchase order, stock transfer, sale order, return, and adjustment.

### Pantry
Manage pantry food/drinks, operating hours, inventory, expiry date, batch, and order flow.

### Equipment / Maintenance
Manage equipment asset, status, maintenance schedule, broken report, maintenance ticket, and cost history.

### CRM / Customer Care
Manage leads, trial follow-up, renewal follow-up, no-show calls, tickets, complaints, and care notes.

### Rating / Feedback
Manage feedback for PT, class, branch, service, equipment, and customer support.

### Promotion / Coupon
Manage coupon, campaign, student discount, referral, and package promotion.

### Notification
Manage email, SMS, push, Zalo, booking reminder, payment reminder, expiry reminder, and campaign notification.

### Report / Analytics
Manage operational, revenue, branch, member, booking, PT, inventory, and conversion reports.

### Audit Log
Record sensitive business actions such as contract, payment, package activation, refund, permission change, KYC approval, and inventory adjustment.
